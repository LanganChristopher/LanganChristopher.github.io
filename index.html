American Sign Language Machine Learning Project

How can we bridge the gap between the Deaf and Hard of Hearing community with the rest of the world? As an ASL user myself, I decided to focus on creating a model that can accurately understand a visual language. This is only the foundation to a much bigger project.

Reddit NLP Project

Drugs are such a taboo subject! I decided to look into the subreddits of Marijuana and LSD/psychadelics to understand what the average users are experiencing. The positive and negatives with certain text as well as some sentiment analysis. I have a big interest in mental health and have been digging into understanding alternatives from pharmaceutical medicine.

Natural Diaster Project

How can we measure the economic impact of a disaster in terms of wages and unemployment? We chose Hurricane Sandy. We created an ARIMA model to assess the sudden fluctuations in the economic data. Check out our presentation

Washington, D.C. Traffic Violation Exploratory Project

A group project where we had to use our exploratory data analysis skills to dive deep into the violations of traffic stops in Washington, D.C. With no specific question to answer, we had to dig around and find anything we can use to present about the specific data we had.
